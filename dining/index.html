After Your Research, A Taste of Tokyo Awaits.

Unwind and dine in Hobo Shinjuku Norengai, an authentic restaurant alley just a short walk from Sugata Research.

The Perfect Post-Research Idea

Just a 5-minute walk from our Sugata Research facility, Hobo Shinjuku Norengai offers a memorable dining experience perfect for entertaining clients. This unique area, composed of renovated traditional Japanese houses, provides an atmospheric setting with a diverse selection of incredible food. Here are a few of our top recommendations.

1. Azzurro520 Yoyogi

Spanish-Italian

Experience fresh seafood with a Spanish-Italian twist. A fantastic wine selection and terrace seating make it a great place to relax.

View on Google Maps

2. Gyutan Iroha Bettei

Charcoal-grilled Beef Tongue

A true Japanese delicacy. This restaurant serves exquisite grilled beef tongue in a calm, traditional setting that your overseas guests are sure to appreciate.

View on Google Maps

3. Awa-tsutsumi Champagne Mania

Champagne & Gyoza

An unexpectedly perfect and trendy pairing. Enjoy handmade gyoza dumplings with premium champagne, including a Moët & Chandon free-flow option.

View on Google Maps

4. Aroi Brothers

Asian Street Food Bar

A lively izakaya-style bar serving delicious charcoal-grilled dishes from Thailand, Vietnam, and Singapore. Perfect for a vibrant and casual evening.

View on Google Maps

5. Unakushi Yakitori U-Fuku

Eel & Chicken Skewers

For an authentic taste of Japanese soul food, this restaurant specializes in perfectly grilled eel skewers (unakushi) and classic yakitori.

View on Google Maps
