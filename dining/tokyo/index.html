Quick tips for overseas guests

  • Reservations: Popular spots fill up. Ask your Seed PM and we’ll take care of it.
  • Allergies/Dietary: Tell us in advance; most places can accommodate.
  • Payment: Cards widely accepted; small spots may prefer cash. Ramen shops are often cash‑only.
  • Tipping: Not required (a service charge may be included).
  • Late arrivals: 10–15 minutes late can be treated as a cancellation at some venues.
Questions? Contact your Seed PM.
