📍 From Hotel to Facility

If it’s your first time at a facility, it’s normal to feel unsure. Some Linkers even do a dry run the day before. On the day, simply show the Google Map address from the Facility Information to your Uber or taxi driver. If you get dropped off in the wrong spot or can’t find the entrance, chat us on Teams immediately and we’ll guide you in.

Heads‑up: Japan taxi drivers prefer an address in Japanese. Your Facility Information includes one — that’s the safest thing to show.

🍱 Meals at the Facility

Depending on headcount, we’ll either pre‑order bento boxes or place a group Uber order so everyone gets what they want. Either way, it’s already covered as project catering — no extra admin for you. Even in the back room, we want you to enjoy good food during your Japan stay.

🚄 Tokyo ↔ Osaka (Shinkansen)

A Seed teammate will accompany you between Tokyo and Osaka. We handle ticket purchase and seat assignments end‑to‑end. Seed will front the cost for you and your accompanying client(s) and later settle via expense reconciliation. Relax — just bring yourselves.

🍽️ Post‑Research Dinner

Too many options? Tell us the style you want — e.g., yakiniku (BBQ), washoku (Japanese), ramen — and we’ll recommend spots and make the reservation if needed. Your clients will feel looked after without you juggling bookings.

🗓️ Free Day Planning

If you score a half or full day before departure, we can plan a relaxed course in Tokyo or Osaka and handle all arrangements. Just say what would make the day feel easy and fun.

💡 Quick Tips

  • Carry some cash (¥10,000–¥20,000). Shrines, stalls, and small shops may be cash‑only.
  • Hydrate in summer — heat can sneak up on you.
  • Ping your Seed PM on Teams anytime. We’re on standby throughout your stay.
